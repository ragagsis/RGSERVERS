✅ AI Prompt Writing Checklist

Use this checklist to write clear and effective prompts for AI tasks
  • 1. Who are you?
    Clearly introduce yourself and your role or context.
    e.g., "I am a Grade 9 student working on a science project."
  • 2. Who is the AI?
    Tell the AI what role it should take.
    e.g., "You are my assistant who helps explain concepts clearly."
  • 3. What tone or style should the AI use?
    Choose how the AI should sound (friendly, formal, fun, academic, poetic, etc).
    e.g., "Use a friendly and encouraging tone."
  • 4. What do you want the AI to do?
    Be specific about the task.
    e.g., "Write a short summary of the French Revolution in 100 words."
  • 5. What format should the answer be in?
    Choose how you want the answer to look (list, paragraph, table, script, code, etc).
    e.g., "Give the answer as a bullet-point list."
  • 6. Any special instructions or rules?
    Mention age group, word count, things to include or avoid.
    e.g., "Use simple language for 10-year-olds. Avoid complicated words."
  • 7. Mention the response length
    Tell it how long it should be.
    e.g., "Your response should include a 150 word paragraph and at least 7 pointers."
  • 8. (Optional) Add an example
    Show what you want by giving a sample or mini example.
    e.g., "Here’s the kind of explanation I like: ‘Imagine you’re a caterpillar...’"
